Demographics

Demography of Rosario

Rosario is one of the densest municipalities in Cavite — home to the pioneering Cavite Economic Zone that drove the province's industrial revolution. In-migration has been continuous since the early 1990s, driven by employment opportunities in the economic zone.

The municipality covers 768.36 hectares with a population density of 147 per hectare. The potential labor force (15 years and above) comprises 72.67% of the total population, mostly engaged in fishing and trade, with a growth rate of 3.53%.
